Relations

Relations show how entities are dependent of each other. An entity cannot only be related to another entity type, but also to another entity of the same kind. E.g. a person can obviously be related to an organization or a location. The corresponding relations can be "... is employee of ..." or "... is living at ..." for those cases. However, a person can also be related to another person, like with the "... is married to ..." relation.

For each entity a self defined set of relations is available with the CDH2's generic data model.
