Getting Started

To access CDH2, enter the following http address into the address line of your Internet browser:

https://<server_name>:<port>/

The port number (<port>) must be specified during the installation process. The installer suggests the value XXXX.

At the first login following the installation of CDH2, you have to create an initial user

Registration and login flow

After starting CDH2, the login screen appears.

Getting_started-registrationLogin 01.en

  • Enter your login ID in the ‘Email-address' field
  • Enter the password in the 'Password' input line
  • Click the ‘Login’ button to log in to the system.

The CDH2 dashboard view will be displayed after a successful log in.

In case that you have forgotten your password, click on this link. You will be guided through a password reset procedure for your account.

Single-Sign-On

The button 'Login with SSO Service <n>' is only visible when a Single-Sign-On procedure was configured by the system administrator. If the button is displayed in the dialog box your individual authentication is already configured. Thus, an access to multiple CDH2 services is granted with only one single log in procedure.

Password reset and change password

If you forgot your password you have to reset the old password and to define a new one. Follow the instructions to change your password.

After clicking on the link 'Forgot your password?' in the 'Login' dialog box the 'Request password reset' dialog is displayed.

  • Enter your Email-Address in the 'Login/E-Mail' line.
  • Click on 'Send request' to proceed.
  • Click on 'Cancel' to abort.

Getting_started-registrationPasswordReset_ 02.en

Once you have requested a new password your request will be confirmed. The dialog box 'Password reset requested' informs you that the password reset process is in progress and that you have been notified about the further procedure by an e-mail.

  • Switch to that e-mail and follow the instructions.
  • Click on 'Back to login' to get back to the 'Login' dialog.
Password reset notification

As a result of your request for resetting your password you will be notified by e-mail about the next steps. The e-mail contains the 'Password reset' dialog.

Click on 'Reset password now!' to proceed.

Getting_started-registrationPasswordReset_ 03.en

After this the 'Reset password now!' button was clicked the 'Reset password' dialog is displayed. Your next steps are:

  • Enter a new Password in the 'New Password' input line (Minimum 6 characters)
  • Retype the new password in the 'Retype new password'.
  • Click on 'Reset to confirm and to get back to the 'Login' dialog.
  • Click on 'Cancel' to abort.
Change password

Apart from the case that a user has forgotten the password and has to reset it, there are other reasons why a user might change the password.

Those reasons could be:

  • The current password exceeded its duration of validity.
  • The user wants to change the password intentionally and purposefully.

Examples:

The system administrator or access manager can setup a time frame for the validity of a password. As soon as this period runs out the system will inform the user, that the password is no longer valid. Concurrently the user will be requested to change the password.

The 'Password expired' dialog opens as soon as a user tries to login again on the day the old password became invalid. With this dialog the system requests a new password.

  • Enter a new password in the 'New Password' input line (Minimum 6 characters)
  • Retype the password in the 'Retype Password' input line
  • Click on 'Change Password' to update the password

  • Click on 'Change Password' to update your password.
  • Click on 'Cancel' to abort.

User account activation

Once you have registered yourself on the CDH2 system, your account needs to be activated. You will be notified by an automatically generated e-mail that an account was created for you. You have to activate this account by answering the activation request in displayed in the e-mail.

  • Click on the button 'Activate account' and follow the instructions on your screen.

Note:

The link behind the 'Activate account' button will only be valid for the next 21 days.

Getting_started-accountActivation_ 01.en

The 'Activate account' dialog appears as soon as you requested an account activation. Your login ID is displayed automatically.

  • Enter your password in the 'Your password' input line
  • Retype your password in the 'Retype password' input line
  • Click on 'Activate'. You will be redirected to the login page afterwards.

System warnings or messages

When you are working on the CDH2 system you are principally entering and receiving data. Both are triggered by requests. Generally you are sending those requests by clicking on a button like, e.g. 'OK', 'Cancel', 'Confirm' or 'Reset' etc. Those requests will be processed by the system and you will get an output of processed information as a result. In some cases the system provides you with a message or a warning instead of a result. In those cases some actions are required from you.

Two types of warnings or messages can appear while you are connected with CDH2:

Info messages

a request is under certain circumstances not completely processed or the processing of the request is interrupted. In either case a certain action is required from you. Either you can simply continue after you took notice of the message or you choose an option out of, at least, two options to proceed. The processing of the request can be continued after this.
Warning messages

a request is not processed because of an error. The request needs to be repeated after solving the cause of the error.

Getting_started-systemWarnings_ 01.en

In the 'Activate account' dialog on the left hand side shows an example for warning message. In this case you entered your password twice and the system informed you that they weren't identical. The reason is probably a spelling error in the input. You have to retype your passwords and click 'Activate' again.

The dialog box in the middle informs you that have been logged out from the system automatically, because the duration of the phase of your inactivity was too long. You have to click on 'Login Again' to restore your access to the system again.

On the right hand side the 'Password expired' dialog is displayed. You will be prompted to enter a new password in the 'New password' input line and to retype the new password in the 'Retype password' input line. Confirm your new password by clicking on 'Change Password'. The changes will be put to effect after a new login with your new password.
