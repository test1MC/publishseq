User profile

The user profile page contains the following sections:

Getting_started-userProfile_ 03.en

Basic user settings

The section 'Basic User Settings' is necessary for the basic configuration of your CDH2.

  • Enter your First (Last-) name in the respective input fields.
  • Choose language, country and the appropriate time zone from the dropdown fields.
  • Click 'Save' to save your entries
  • Click 'Revert changes' to wipe of the input fields in this section

Once you have saved your changes the message 'User Profile successfully updated' is displayed.

Getting_started-userBasicSettings_ 01.en

First (Last-) name

Enter a first and a last into these input fields

Language & country

Choose a language / country combination from the list field. The entries in the dropdown field are sorted alphabetically according to the language. Your choice defines your preferred working language and predefines the settings in the time zone field.

Time zone

Choose the time zone of the location of your working place from the dropdown field. This entry is necessary to set time stamps for each event triggered by users for their respective local time. The entries are sorted alphabetically by principal cities like Amsterdam, Berlin, Paris or Yaund√©. These cities are representing the 'UTC +01:00' time zone. The 'UTC + 01:00' time zone is identical with CET (Central European Time) or WAT (West Africa Time).
Login credentials

In the section 'Login Credentials' your login ID and your password are available. You can change your password using this section.

Click on 'Change' for changing your password. This will direct you to the 'Change password' dialog (see also 'Change password')

Getting_started-userLoginCredentials_ 01.en

Account information

The 'Account information' section shows some additional information about your account. These settings cannot be changed without administrator rights.

Getting_started-userAccountInformation_ 01.en

Assigned roles

in this sub-section all roles are displayed, that were assigned to your account. Each role allows you to perform a range of actions that can be performed on the system. The roles appear as tiles, however they cannot be changed.

Assigned teams

at least one team might be assigned to your account. It is displayed in this sub-section.

Account expiration

date when the validity of your account expires. After this date a new account needs to be created for further access.
Active login sessions

A table with an overview of all active login sessions is displayed in the 'Active Login Sessions' section of the 'User Profile' page. Several detailed information about all currently active browser sessions is displayed here. You can also terminate a session.

  • Click on a 'Skip' button that is visible at the end of a session line.
  • In the reply the system will ask you "Do you really want to terminate this session?"
  • Click on 'Terminate' to terminate the session immediately.
  • Click on 'Cancel' to abort.

Getting_started-userLoginSessions_ 01.en

Device

in this column the used browser, the browser version and the respective operating system are displayed for each active login session

Last login date

the last login date is displayed in this column. Depending on your localization information the format will be "YYYY-MM-DD hh:mm".

Location

the location of the respective login.

Note:

If a location or the operating system cannot be detected a "-" is displayed.
