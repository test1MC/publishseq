Task management

Tasks are always related to records.

This means that records are passing the validation, matching, merge and propagation phase during a single work flow . After each phase the system evaluates the records and decides whether a record passes or not. Flawed records won't pass and will be labeled for getting reviewed. That is a task. The flawed tasks will be categorized automatically for a post processing phase.

All tasks are objects of certain categories

The Category of a task can consist of the respective phase, where the related issue occurred, and the entity that caused the issue. Those tasks are automatically generated by the system. Besides, additional tasks can be generated because of a users intervention.
