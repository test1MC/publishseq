Labels & Tags

Labels and tags are useful ways of marking, segmenting or categorizing data. Labels can be seen as a predefined list of tags, which can be assigned to a record or a task. Tags are single words (not predefined), which can be also assigned to a record or a task.

A label appears with a colored sign (symbol) for certain types of issues. However, this doesn't apply for tags. Tags are entirely user defined.
